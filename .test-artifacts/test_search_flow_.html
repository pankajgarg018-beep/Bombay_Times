Aishwarya Rai Bachchan Dazzles at Cannes 2026 in Bold Blue Couture and Soft Pink Glam, Serves Regal Elegance With Daughter Aaradhya

Aishwarya Rai Bachchan made a stunning return to Cannes 2026 with two glamorous fashion moments alongside daughter Aaradhya Bachchan. From dramatic red carpet style to elegant mother-daughter appearances, the actor once again owned the spotlight.
Rishika SinghMay 23, 2026, 09:54 IST

Aishwarya Rai Bachchan has been walking the Cannes red carpet since 2002 and yet every year, the internet behaves as though it is seeing her there for the very first time. This year was no different. The actress, attending Cannes 2026 as a big brand's global ambassador, arrived at the French Riviera with daughter Aaradhya Bachchan in tow and together the two gave fans more than they had bargained for.

Before her arrival, there was no shortage of speculation online. Aishwarya's name and face were missing from official campaign hoardings at Hotel Martinez, which set off a wave of concern among fans. The brand later responded, calling her a living legend. She then answered everyone herself by simply showing up, twice and looking extraordinary both times.

Aishwarya Rai's Cannes 2026 Look 1: Royal Blue Couture

For her first Cannes appearance this year, Aishwarya wore a structured royal blue gown by Amit Aggarwal, named Luminara. The piece featured sculptural shoulder detailing, Crystal Vein embroidery and a mermaid silhouette. She paired it with a sheen dupatta and sapphire jewellery. Styled by Mohit Rai, her hair was worn in long waves with a side sweep and her makeup leaned into bold eyes and rosy lips. The designer said the creation was built around the concept of light as energy and transformation and on Aishwarya, it read exactly that way.

Aishwarya Rai's Cannes 2026 Look 2: Pink Couture And Aaradhya's Big Moment

At the L'Oreal Lights On Women's Worth event, Aishwarya appeared in a pastel pink Sophie Couture gown with a corseted bodice, Fortuny pleating, Swarovski floral detailing and a flowing cape. The look was soft and romantic, a sharp contrast to the evening's earlier drama. Aaradhya, 14, joined her mother in a deep ruby-red gown with a cape detail and loose curls. Social media could not stop talking. Fans called it a full-circle Cannes moment, with many noting how closely Aaradhya resembles her mother.

Aishwarya Rai has attended Cannes for over two decades now, and she still manages to make each visit feel like a moment. This year, she did not walk the red carpet alone in the spotlight. She brought her daughter along, and together, they reminded everyone why this family holds a very particular place in India's relationship with Cannes.​
